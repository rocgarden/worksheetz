Name:___________________________  Nouns  A singular noun names one person, place, thing, or idea. A plural noun names more than one person, place, thing, or idea. Complete sentences are made up of one or more singular or plural nouns.  Examples of   singular   nouns are: day, dog, she, country  Examples of   plural   nouns are: children, days, they  Example: A breed of cat known for having seven toes is called a "pixiebob ” .
